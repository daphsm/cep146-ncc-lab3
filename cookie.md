# Cookie Recipe

## Ingredients
- Butter
- Sugars
- Flour
- Salt
- Chocolate Chips

## Step

1. Melt the butter
2. Whisk in the sugars
3. Mix in the vanilla extract and egg
4. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt
5. Stir in the chocolate chips
6. Bake for about 7-10 minutes and adjust the temp to your liking
7. Serve!
