# Appetizer
This is a simple and easy nacho recipe
## Ingredients
- Tortilla Chips
- Cheese
- Toppings

## Steps
1. On a baking sheet, start with the tortilla chips
2. Put a generous amount of cheese
3. Time to start adding your preffered toppings
4. Bake the nachos between 3-15 minutes for 175 degrees celsius
5. Serve
